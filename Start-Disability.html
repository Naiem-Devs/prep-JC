This policy provides disability income insurance only. It does NOT provide basic hospital, basic medical, or major medical insurance as defined by the New York State Department of Financial Services.

A Disability that begins during the first 12 months of coverage and is due to a pre-existing condition* is excluded.

Elimination period is the length of time from the beginning of an injury or illness until payable benefits begin. Longer elimination periods offer a lower premium cost per paycheck.

**A pre-existing condition is an injury or sickness for which you received medical treatment, consultation, diagnostic measures, or prescribed drugs or medicines or followed treatment recommendations during the 3 months prior to your effective date of Disability coverage or 6 months prior to your effective date of Critical Illness coverage.
